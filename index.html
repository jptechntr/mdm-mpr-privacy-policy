Privacy Policy

MDM MPR is a utility application designed to assist users in calculation and report preparation related to the PM POSHAN (Mid-Day Meal) program.

Data Collection and Storage

This application primarily stores data locally on the user’s device. However, to provide online features such as data backup and multi-device synchronization, certain data may be transmitted to and stored on our secure cloud servers after user authentication.

User Support and Communication

To provide real-time support and resolve technical queries, we integrate a built-in chat service. Any information shared during a chat session (such as text, screenshots, or contact details) is used solely to assist you and resolve your issues. This data is stored securely on our infrastructure.

Advertising and Analytics

To keep this service free, we use third-party advertising services. We integrate Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use your device's Advertising ID to show relevant ads. We also use Firebase Remote Config to manage app configurations and ad placements dynamically.

Internet Usage

The application requires internet connectivity to sync user data, receive remote configurations, display advertisements, facilitate real-time support chat, and open official government website links in the user’s default browser for informational updates.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to improve your experience:

  • AdMob (for Advertisements)
  • Firebase (for Remote Config and Cloud Storage)
